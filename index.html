How to Install Games on Your TI-84 Plus CE

  1. Step 1: Download TI Connect CE software from the Texas Instruments website.

  2. Step 2: Connect your calculator to your computer using a USB cable.

  3. Step 3: Open TI Connect CE and drag the downloaded game files (.8xp or .8xk) onto your calculator.

  4. Step 4: Safely disconnect your calculator.

  5. Step 5: On your calculator, open the PRGM or Apps menu and select the game to start playing!
